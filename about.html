Xiarisse Tan

Photographer / Web Designer & Developer

I'm a freelance photographer. I take pictures of people and nature.
Born and finished my university in the Philippines before migrating to Canada in 2015.
My goal is to show the beauty, grace, strength and enthusiasm of people.
I focus on non-models and place regular people in exaggerated situations that highlight
an aspect of who they are. I look for a sense of defiance and fortitude in my work.
